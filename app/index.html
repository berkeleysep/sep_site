Hi! I'm Anoushka, the current president of SEP. I am a junior studying film and cognitive science. I am passionate about telling stories in any way I can: through writing, through film and through dance. My strongest belief is that entrepreneurship is inherently creative, and that is what draws me to it.

SEP has given me a community of innovators: people with distinct interests and skills, and most importantly, people who are always ready to create. They've introduced me to new skills and helped me better my old ones. It doesn't matter what your major is: if you have a desire to innovate, SEP is the place for you!
