FAQ

What is the pledging process?

Our pledging process is a semester long and is centered around professional and entrepreneurial development, personal growth, and brotherhood. While pledging, you will apply what you learn through the ventures you choose to pursue. We provide our pledges with a strong curriculum and professional development backbone as well as opportunities for them to get to know their pledge class and the active house on a personal level.

What kind of people make up your current community?

About 50% of our members are technical majors (Computer Science, Cognitive Science, Chemical Engineering, etc.), while 50% are non-technical majors (Business, Rhetoric, Political Science, Pyschology, etc.).

What does SEP look for in applicants?

We are looking for people who are:

  • passionate about their interests
  • ready to jump into a semester of exploring entrepreneurship hands-on
  • willing to challenge both the status quo and themselves

We are NOT looking for:

  • only people from a specific background/major
  • only people specifically with a lot of entrepreneurship experience in the past (although you are also more than welcome!)

Your life experiences are enough to equip you to solve unique problems that exist in the world today. Diversity strengthens entrepreneurship and community.
